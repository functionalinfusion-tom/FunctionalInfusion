Recall & Quality Policy

Document Reference: Functional Infusion Product Recall Plan  |  Last updated: April 2026

Functional Infusion operates as a B2B co-manufacturing and co-packing facility. All services are provided on a contract manufacturing basis. The following policy governs product quality claims and recall procedures.

Scope

This policy applies to all gummies, beverages, and functional shots manufactured or packaged at our Waupaca, WI facility.

Manufacturing Services

Because production runs involve perishable ingredients, customized formulations, and brand-specific packaging, Functional Infusion does not offer refunds on completed production runs. All production is performed to the specifications agreed upon in the signed co-packing agreement.

Quality Claims

If you believe a production run did not meet agreed quality specifications, you must notify Functional Infusion in writing within 5 business days of receiving finished goods. Claims must include:

  • Lot code(s) in question
  • A description of the non-conformance
  • Supporting documentation (photos, COA discrepancy, etc.)

Functional Infusion will investigate all quality claims and, where a manufacturing defect is confirmed, will offer one of the following at our discretion: re-run at no cost, partial credit toward a future run, or prorated refund on affected units.

Product Recall Procedures

Recalls may be initiated due to: test results, supplier recalls, labeling errors, packaging failures, consumer complaints, or regulatory notification.

Traceability

All lot numbers are assigned and tracked in our system. Each batch is logged digitally and in physical binders. Affected lot records can be retrieved within 4 hours of a recall determination.

Recall Hold Area

A designated Recall Hold Area is maintained within our finished goods storage to segregate affected products immediately upon recall initiation.

Regulatory Notification

Functional Infusion will notify DATCP and/or the FDA within 24 hours of a recall determination. Brand owners will be notified concurrently by our Operations Manager.

Recall Coordinator

Tom Link, CEO  |  Backup: Dana Howard, Plant Foreman

Product Disposition

All recalled product will be destroyed with full documentation.

Cancellations

Production run cancellations must be submitted in writing. Cancellations received after materials have been ordered or staged may be subject to restocking, material, and scheduling fees.

Record Retention

Physical records are retained for a minimum of 2 years. Digital records are retained for a minimum of 10 years.
