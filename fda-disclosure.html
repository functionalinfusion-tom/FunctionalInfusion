FDA Disclosure

Last updated: March 2026

IMPORTANT NOTICE: The statements and products described on this website have not been evaluated by the Food and Drug Administration (FDA). Products manufactured by Functional Infusion are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or health condition.

Co-Manufacturing Disclosure

Functional Infusion is a co-manufacturing and co-packing facility. We produce finished goods to client specifications. The formulations, label claims, health claims, and marketing language associated with finished products are the sole responsibility of the brand owner (our client). Functional Infusion does not make health claims on behalf of any product we manufacture.

Functional Ingredients

Some products manufactured at our facility may contain functional ingredients including but not limited to: hemp-derived cannabinoids (CBD, Delta-9 THC), adaptogenic botanicals, vitamins and minerals, nootropics, and other bioactive compounds. The regulatory status of these ingredients varies by jurisdiction.

Hemp-Derived Cannabinoids

Products containing hemp-derived cannabinoids are manufactured in accordance with applicable federal and state law. Hemp-derived Delta-9 THC at or below 0.3% on a dry weight basis is federally legal under the 2018 Farm Bill. State laws vary. It is the responsibility of the brand owner and end consumer to understand and comply with local regulations.

Age Restriction

Certain products manufactured at our facility — including those containing alcohol or cannabinoids — are intended for adults 21 years of age or older. Brand owners are responsible for implementing appropriate age verification in their sales and marketing channels.

Consult a Professional

Consumers should consult a qualified healthcare professional before using any product containing functional ingredients, particularly if pregnant, nursing, taking medications, or managing a health condition.
