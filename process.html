Production Flow

Our 6-Step Production Process

01

Intake & Inspection

All raw materials are received, inspected, and logged against your approved ingredient list. Non-conforming materials are quarantined and rejected before they can affect your run.

Intake & Inspection
02

Formulation & QC Check

Your formula is staged per batch record. Pre-production QC confirms Brix, pH, viscosity, or weight targets depending on product type. Nothing moves to production until targets are confirmed.

Formulation & QC
03

Production Run

Filling, sealing, capping, or molding executed on calibrated equipment with in-line monitoring. Operators document every deviation. Consistency is the product.

Production Run
04

Quality Hold & Release

Finished goods are held pending QC sign-off. Certificate of Analysis available on request. No product ships before release — full stop.

Quality Hold & Release
05

Labeling & Packaging

Auto or hand-applied labels. Primary and secondary packaging per your spec sheet. Lot codes applied to every case so traceability never breaks.

Labeling & Packaging
06

Fulfillment & Dispatch

3PL pick-and-pack, pallet storage, or full outbound LTL/parcel shipping. Inventory tracked by lot and SKU. Your product ships on your schedule.

Fulfillment & Dispatch
