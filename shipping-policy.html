Shipping Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Functional Infusion provides 3PL fulfillment services including pick-and-pack, pallet storage, and outbound shipping for co-packed finished goods. The following terms apply to all outbound fulfillment services.

Outbound Fulfillment

  • Pick & Pack fee: $1.50 per order
  • Pallet storage: $22/pallet/month
  • Shipping charges are billed at cost (carrier invoice + handling); no markup on freight unless otherwise agreed in writing

Carriers

Functional Infusion ships via UPS, FedEx, and LTL freight carriers. Carrier selection is based on shipment size, destination, and client preference. Clients may supply their own carrier account numbers for outbound shipments.

Processing Times

Standard order processing is 2–3 business days after quality release of finished goods. Rush fulfillment may be available for an additional fee — contact us to confirm availability before committing to rush timelines.

Pallet & LTL Shipments

Full pallet and LTL shipments require a scheduled pickup window. Functional Infusion will coordinate with your carrier or our preferred freight partners. Delivery windows are estimates only and are subject to carrier availability.

Damaged Shipments

If goods arrive damaged, document the damage at time of delivery and contact us within 48 hours. Retain original packaging for carrier claims. Functional Infusion will assist with carrier claim documentation but is not responsible for carrier-caused damage once goods leave our facility.

International Shipping

International fulfillment is available on a case-by-case basis. Client is responsible for import duties, customs documentation, and compliance with destination country regulations. Contact us before placing an international fulfillment order.
