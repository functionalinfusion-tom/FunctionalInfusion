Return Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Functional Infusion operates as a B2B co-manufacturing and co-packing facility. All services are provided on a contract manufacturing basis. The following policy governs service-related disputes and product quality claims.

Manufacturing Services

Because production runs involve perishable ingredients, customized formulations, and brand-specific packaging, Functional Infusion does not offer refunds on completed production runs. All production is performed to the specifications agreed upon in the signed co-packing agreement.

Quality Claims

If you believe a production run did not meet the agreed quality specifications, you must notify Functional Infusion in writing within 5 business days of receiving finished goods. Claims must include:

  • Lot code(s) in question
  • Description of the non-conformance
  • Supporting documentation (photos, COA discrepancy, etc.)

Functional Infusion will investigate all quality claims and, where a manufacturing defect is confirmed, will offer one of the following remedies at our discretion: re-run at no cost, partial credit toward a future run, or prorated refund on affected units.

Raw Materials & Inventory

Client-supplied raw materials are accepted for use in production only. Functional Infusion is not responsible for loss, spoilage, or damage to client-supplied ingredients beyond normal care and storage. Client materials stored at our facility are subject to our standard storage terms.

Cancellations

Production run cancellations must be submitted in writing. Cancellations received after materials have been ordered or staged for production may be subject to restocking, material, and scheduling fees.
