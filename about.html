Our Story

From Hemp Soda to Full-Service Co-Packing

Functional Infusion started as a hemp-infused soda tolling operation — small batches, big ambitions, and a deep belief that functional beverages deserved a manufacturing partner as intentional as the brands making them. That early work taught us the fundamentals: traceability matters, documentation is protection, and every client's formula deserves the same precision regardless of run size.

That foundation grew into a full-service co-packing operation. Today we run three dedicated production lines — beverage canning, functional shot filling, and gummy manufacturing — with documented SOPs on every run and full lot-level traceability from raw ingredient intake to outbound pallet.

"Simple Ingredients. Naturally Functional." isn't just a tagline — it's an operating principle. Every SOP we write, every QC record we keep, every batch we run reflects that commitment. We don't take shortcuts, and we don't work with brands that do.

"Your brand's name is on the label.
Our process is what protects it."

Waupaca, Wisconsin is centrally located for Midwest freight — a deliberate choice that gives our clients shipping cost advantages whether they're moving product to Chicago, Minneapolis, or the coasts. The facility was designed from day one for scale: not just for where brands start, but for where they're going.
